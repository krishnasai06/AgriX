üíß REAL TIME- MONITORING

How It Works:

Sensors in the Soil:

Special moisture sensors are installed at the root level of your crops. These can measure how wet or dry the soil is.

Wireless Data Transmission:

The sensor sends this information wirelessly (via Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or cellular networks) to a cloud platform or a local device.

Real-Time Alerts:

If the soil gets too dry or too wet, the system can notify you instantly via SMS, app notification, or email.

Mobile Dashboard:

You can view real-time charts and data trends over days or weeks, helping you decide exactly when to water.










üìä USAGE TRACKING

How It Works:

Water Flow Sensors:

Devices installed in your water pipeline measure the volume of water flowing to your crops in real-time.

Electricity Meters:

These track the energy consumed by motors or pumps powering your irrigation, helping you monitor power usage.

Data Logging:

All data is recorded and stored for daily, weekly, or monthly analysis.

Reports & Alerts:

You get detailed reports on water and electricity usage, which help identify inefficiencies, leaks, or unusual consumption patterns.
